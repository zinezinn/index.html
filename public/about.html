About howth

Howth Head was immortalised in James Joyce's ulysses, and its not difficult to see why - prose,poetry and song are worthy tributes to the coastal beauty of the area. originally a small fishing village, howth has grown to be a major coastal area with a busy fishing port. the town,with its maritime influences and historics roots, is full of variety,with wild moorland, and stunning cliff and coastal wallks with easy reach.
